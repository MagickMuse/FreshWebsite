Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Magick Manuscript built in?

The Magick Manuscript is built entirely in Notion. You need a free Notion account to access it. The Full Manuscript and Lifetime versions give you view and comment access. The Blank Template can be duplicated directly into your own Notion workspace.

Can I edit and customize the manuscript?

The Full Manuscript and Lifetime versions are view and comment access, meaning you can read every entry and add personal comments, but the core content is not editable. The Blank Template is fully yours once you duplicate it, so you can build and customize it completely.

What's the difference between Full and Lifetime?

Full Manuscript gives you everything currently in the manuscript, including updates released during your access period. Lifetime Access gives you every future update, expansion, and new grimoire added, permanently, with no additional cost ever.

How is the Blank Template different from the Full Manuscript?

The Blank Template has the complete architecture, all fifteen grimoires, all database relationships, all organizational structure, but with no pre-filled entries. It is for practitioners who want to build their own content within our framework, and it can be duplicated to your own Notion workspace.

Is there a refund policy?

Because the manuscript is a digital product, all sales are final. We encourage you to explore the Free Version first to ensure the manuscript is the right fit for your practice.

Do I need to know Notion to use the manuscript?

Basic Notion familiarity is helpful but not required. The manuscript is designed to be immediately usable. The Discord community also includes resources for getting comfortable with Notion if you're new to it.
