Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Magick Manuscript built in?

The Magick Manuscript is built entirely in Notion. You need a free Notion account to access it. Monthly Access and Lifetime Access give you view and comment access. The Blank Manuscript can be duplicated directly into your own Notion workspace.

Can I edit and customize the manuscript?

Monthly Access and Lifetime Access are view and comment access, meaning you can read every entry and add personal comments, but the core content is not editable. The Blank Manuscript is fully yours once you duplicate it, so you can build and customize it completely.

What's the difference between Monthly and Lifetime?

Monthly Access gives you everything currently in the manuscript, including updates released during your subscription. Lifetime Access gives you every future update, expansion, and new grimoire added, permanently, with no additional cost ever.

How is the Blank Manuscript different from Monthly or Lifetime Access?

The Blank Manuscript has the complete architecture, all fifteen grimoires, all database relationships, all organizational structure, but with no pre-filled entries. It is for practitioners who want to build their own content within our framework, and it can be duplicated to your own Notion workspace.

Is there a refund policy?

Because the manuscript is a digital product, all sales are final. We encourage you to explore the Free Crystal Grimoire first to ensure the manuscript is the right fit for your practice.

Do I need to know Notion to use the manuscript?

Basic Notion familiarity is helpful but not required. The manuscript is designed to be immediately usable. The Discord community also includes resources for getting comfortable with Notion if you're new to it.
