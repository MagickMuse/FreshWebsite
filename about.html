The Creator

A Witch Who Needed Better Tools

✦

The Magick Muse began with a frustration familiar to any serious practitioner: witchcraft knowledge is scattered. Across books, websites, forums, and personal journals, and none of it talks to itself.

The creator of The Magick Muse is a practicing witch with years of experience across multiple traditions. The journey through folk magick, traditional witchcraft, and eclectic practice created a deep appreciation for the breadth of magickal knowledge, and an equally deep frustration with how poorly organized most of that knowledge is.

The Magick Manuscript started as a personal project: a Notion database built to organize what had been learned over years of practice. What began as a private tool became something that needed to be shared.

How It Began

The Origins of the Magick Manuscript

✦

It started with crystals. A simple database to track which crystals had been acquired, what their properties were, and how to use them. But crystals connect to planets, planets connect to herbs, herbs connect to rituals. The database kept growing.

Within months, what had begun as a crystal tracker became the seed of something much larger, a genuine attempt to map the entire landscape of practical witchcraft knowledge in a single, interconnected system.

The decision to share it publicly came from conversations in the witchcraft community, from the constant realization that other practitioners needed exactly this, and that keeping it private was a waste of something valuable.

The Magick Muse became the home for the manuscript and everything built around it: the guides, the community, and the ongoing project of making witchcraft knowledge as organized and accessible as it deserves to be.
