About the Community

More Than a Server. A Coven.

The Magick Muse Discord is built around a simple belief: that witches are better when they learn together. Not in a shallow way, but through real discussion, shared experience, and genuine curiosity.

This is not a space for gatekeeping. All paths are welcome, all experience levels are honored, and all genuine questions deserve thoughtful answers. What we ask for in return is respect, sincerity, and a willingness to engage with depth.

The community is also the living companion to the Magick Manuscript, where new content is announced, where members can request new entries, and where the manuscript evolves based on the needs of real practitioners.

🤝

What's Inside

What the Discord Offers

Structured channels designed to support every dimension of magickal practice and community life.

💬

Deep Discussion

Dedicated channels for discussing magickal theory, tradition, philosophy, and the practical realities of keeping a witchcraft practice alive. Conversations go places you won't find in books.

🕯️

Spell & Working Sharing

Share your rituals, workings, and spells. Get feedback, offer yours, and see how other practitioners approach similar intentions. Learn from what actually works in real practice.

🌙

Advice & Learning

Ask questions without judgment. Whether you're wondering about your first altar or deepening an established practice, the community is here to offer thoughtful, grounded guidance.

🤲

Community Support

Witchcraft can be a solitary path. The Discord is a reminder that you don't have to walk it alone. Find people who understand the craft and the commitment it takes to practice seriously.

✨

Manuscript Updates

New entries, expanded grimoires, and corrections are announced in the Discord first. Members can also request topics, report gaps, and shape the direction of future manuscript content.

📚

Resource Sharing

Books, articles, videos, and personal recommendations, members share the resources that have genuinely helped their practice, building a living library alongside the manuscript.

Community Values

What We Stand For

⚖️

Respect Across Traditions

Wicca, traditional witchcraft, folk magick, eclectic practice, all paths are welcome here. We don't believe there is one right way to practice.

🎓

Depth Over Performance

We're here for genuine learning and honest conversation, not aesthetic, social performance, or surface-level content. Depth is valued.

🌱

All Experience Levels Welcome

Beginners have questions. Advanced practitioners have answers. Both are essential. No one is made to feel small for where they are in their journey.

🔒

A Managed, Safe Space

The Discord is actively moderated to maintain a respectful, safe environment. Gatekeeping, harassment, and bad faith engagement are not tolerated.
